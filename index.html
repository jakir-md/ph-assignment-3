Frequently Asked Questions

What are the steps to buying a home?

This typically includes getting pre-approved for a mortgage, finding a real estate agent, viewing homes, making an offer, getting an inspection, and closing. The timeline varies based on market conditions, but it typically takes 30-60 days from offer to close. The process may be quicker in a fast-moving market or longer if complexities arise.

How do I know how much I can afford to spend on a home?

Financial institutions often suggest spending no more than 30% of your monthly income on housing, but getting a mortgage pre-approval is also a helpful way to determine your budget.

What should I look for during a home inspection?

Key areas include the roof, foundation, plumbing, electrical systems, and overall structural integrity. It's also wise to consider the age and condition of appliances.
